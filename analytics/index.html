Install on Your Phone

Get the native-app experience by installing Zinaka Analytics directly to your home screen.

For Android (Chrome):

Tap the three-dot menu in the top-right corner and select "Install app".

For iPhone (Safari):

Tap the "Share" icon at the bottom of the screen and select "Add to Home Screen".

Add a Transaction

Quickly record your income and expenses with a clean, mobile-first design.

Simply choose a ledger, enter the amount, and assign it as income or an expense. Add a description to keep your records organised and easy to understand.

View & Edit History

Drill down into any ledger to see a full transaction history.

Instantly see the net profit for an individual business or project. Scroll through a clear, chronological list of all income and expenses. Tap on any transaction to edit its details.

Delete Transactions or Ledgers

Keep your finances tidy by removing old or incorrect entries.

You have full control over your data. Easily delete individual transactions from within a ledger, or remove entire ledgers from your main dashboard.
